Our Thesis

Why travel. Why now.

We chose to do one thing, travel and hospitality, and to do all of it.

Travel is the most demanding software domain we know: real-time inventory, razor margins, forty-year-old plumbing, and an industry that never closes. It rewards depth and punishes generalists. So we chose to do one thing, travel and hospitality, and to do all of it, from the boardroom thesis to the system that runs the business.

Now the ground is moving faster than at any point since the GDS era. Agentic commerce is becoming a real channel. NDC and Offer-and-Order are dismantling EDIFACT. Loyalty is a balance-sheet question. The firms that win will be the ones whose systems are already agent-ready, and that is exactly the work we were built to do.
